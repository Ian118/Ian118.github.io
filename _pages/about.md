Howdy!  
My name is Ian Lansdowne, and I'm a Research Engineer for [RAD Lab](https://rad.engr.tamu.edu). I recently graduated from Texas A&M University with my BS in Mechanical Engineering. During my undergrad, I was the president of the largest robotics organization at Texas A&M, [TURTLE Robotics](https://turtlerobotics.org), and I worked for NASA (ER3 and ER4) and RAD Lab. My interest revolve around developing robotic systems, including robots for space and biomimetic robotics.

Please look around my site and see my [past work](/portfolio) and [publications](/publications).
